CAMPAIGN BRIEF

The Grand Tour is an Imperial investigative campaign set in the Solomani Rim. The player characters operate under noble and bureaucratic authority, traveling by yacht to expose corruption, hidden plots, and abuses of power among those who believe status places them beyond justice.

Current Base: Dingir, Solomani Rim

Allegiance: Third Imperium

Primary Mission: Investigate corrupt nobles, officials, and covert threats

Operational Cover: Imperial administrative inspection tour

ACTIVE CASES
