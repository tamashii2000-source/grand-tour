Background

Hazuren Omadim began his career as a performer, specializing in storytelling and social presentation. His talents eventually brought him into the orbit of high society, where he gained a noble patron on Aursis at age 22.

By age 26, Hazuren had befriended a bounty hunter in the Dingir Subsector who sometimes acted as his bodyguard. At age 30, he encountered a psionic institute and was tested, but was determined not to be psionic.

Campaign Use

Hazuren is not merely a servant. He is a trained social operator, performer, purser, and observer of noble society. Aboard the Amelia's Grace, he manages comfort, etiquette, guests, schedules, meals, and appearances.

In an investigation, Hazuren is especially useful at formal dinners, noble receptions, private salons, and anywhere a polite servant can overhear more than the guests realize.
