Imperial Survey Summary

Nergal serves as the primary military world of the Dingir system. The world contains naval supply depots, training facilities, fleet maintenance yards, munitions factories, and defense contractors responsible for supplying both the Imperial Navy and local defense forces.

While Marduk produces civilian industrial goods, Nergal focuses on military hardware, weapons systems, sensors, armored vehicles, and naval support equipment. The world maintains a strong security presence and closely monitors all incoming and outgoing traffic.

Major Locations

  • Fort Victory — Planetary headquarters of the Dingir Defense Command.
  • Naval Support Yard 6 — Primary repair and refit facility for Imperial vessels.
  • The Arsenal District — Massive weapons manufacturing complex.
  • Red Mesa Training Grounds — Military exercises and live-fire operations.
  • The Bastion — Hardened command bunker capable of coordinating system-wide defense operations.

Military Significance

Nergal acts as the logistical backbone of Dingir's defense network. Military personnel make up a substantial portion of the population. Many civilians work directly for defense contractors or support industries.

The world hosts regular naval exercises and serves as a staging area for operations throughout the Solomani Rim.

Political Situation

Security concerns dominate local politics. Civilian authorities exist, but major decisions often require consultation with military leadership. The world's economy depends heavily on Imperial defense spending.

Several intelligence agencies maintain offices on Nergal, making it a center for both legitimate security operations and covert political maneuvering.
