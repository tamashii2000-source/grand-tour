Imperial Survey Summary

Marduk is the industrial heart of the Dingir system. Vast manufacturing complexes, orbital construction yards, refinery districts, and heavy fabrication plants dominate the populated regions of the world.

Nearly every major world in the system depends on Marduk for machinery, industrial components, vehicle parts, environmental systems, and starship equipment. While Dingir serves as the administrative center of the system, Marduk is its economic engine.

Major Locations

  • Foundry Prime — Largest industrial city on the planet.
  • Orbital Yard 14 — Construction and repair facility for civilian spacecraft.
  • The Smoke Sea — Massive industrial district visible from orbit.
  • Titan Works — Heavy machinery and mining equipment manufacturer.
  • The Ring Elevator — Orbital elevator connecting the surface to industrial stations.

Economy

Marduk exports industrial goods throughout the Dingir system and beyond. Major industries include:

  • Starship Components
  • Industrial Machinery
  • Fusion Plant Equipment
  • Atmospheric Processing Systems
  • Mining Equipment
  • Construction Materials

Political Situation

The world is dominated by powerful industrial combines whose influence often rivals that of local government. Labor unions, corporate security forces, and competing manufacturers constantly struggle for advantage.

While generally stable, industrial disputes occasionally escalate into sabotage, strikes, or outright violence.

Adventure Hooks

  • A major factory explosion appears to have been deliberate sabotage.
  • A noble house secretly owns controlling shares in several industrial combines.
  • Workers have disappeared while investigating dangerous conditions in the Smoke Sea.
  • A prototype starship drive vanishes from Orbital Yard 14.
  • An AI-controlled production line has begun manufacturing unauthorized equipment.
  • Evidence suggests Solomani agents are infiltrating industrial contractors.

Rumors

  • Several factories conceal illegal military production lines.
  • The Ring Elevator contains hidden facilities inaccessible to ordinary workers.
  • A forgotten automated factory continues operating beneath Foundry Prime.
  • Corporate executives maintain a private intelligence network rivaling Imperial agencies.
  • Marduk's industrial output is significantly higher than official reports indicate.
