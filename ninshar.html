Imperial Survey Summary

Ninshar is the commercial crossroads of the Anu moon system. It sits astride major fuel, cargo, and passenger routes connecting Dingir, Anu, Marduk, Nergal, and the outer worlds.

No single government controls the moon. Ninshar is divided among rival states, city-domes, merchant leagues, noble holdings, corporate districts, and freeport zones. Competition has made the moon wealthy, but also unstable.

Economy

Ninshar handles banking, shipping contracts, customs disputes, insurance, cargo brokerage, commodity speculation, and legal arbitration for much of the Dingir system.

Nearly every major shipment passing through the Anu fuel network touches Ninshar paperwork, Ninshar money, or Ninshar brokers.

Law & Jurisdiction

Jurisdiction on Ninshar changes from district to district. A crime committed in one dome may fall under an entirely different court than a crime committed two streets away.

This patchwork legal structure creates opportunities for smugglers, corrupt officials, intelligence agents, and wealthy patrons who know which borders to cross and which judges to bribe.
