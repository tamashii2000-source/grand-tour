Emana Zulu is an uplifted chimpanzee and former naval engineering technician serving as lead engineer aboard the Amelia's Grace. Her physical strength is useful in shipboard repair work, but her real value is technical breadth: she is professionally competent across multiple major ship systems.

When not actively working, Emana tends to keep to herself. Crew records describe her as quiet, capable, and difficult to impress.

Profile Notes

As an engineer, Emana has become a professional in all shipboard systems, a level of breadth few engineers ever achieve. This is the primary reason she holds the position of lead engineer aboard the Amelia's Grace.

Her competence also tends to silence suggestions that she was recruited merely for appearances or to make the crew look favorable to a wide variety of species.

"Few engineers know one system perfectly. Zulu knows enough of all of them to keep a ship alive."
