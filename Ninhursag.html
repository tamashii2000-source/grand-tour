Imperial Survey Summary

Ninhursag is a frozen world located in the outer reaches of the Dingir system. Most of its surface is covered by ancient glaciers and frozen oceans. No permanent settlements currently exist.

Automated Imperial probes occasionally monitor the world, but no commercial activity of significance has been recorded in the last century.

Points of Interest

  • The Glass Plains — thousands of square kilometers of perfectly smooth frozen terrain.
  • The White Rift — a canyon system extending over 1,800 kilometers beneath the ice.
  • The Silent Poles — unusual magnetic anomalies interfere with sensors and communications.
  • Survey Station Theta-9 — abandoned Imperial weather monitoring facility.

Rumors

  • Prospectors claim to have discovered alien structures beneath the polar ice.
  • Several survey teams have disappeared while exploring the White Rift.
  • Long-range sensors occasionally detect unexplained energy emissions beneath the crust.
  • Some Imperial Intelligence analysts believe the world was once inhabited.

Adventure Hooks

  • Investigate a distress beacon originating from Survey Station Theta-9.
  • Recover data cores from a crashed Imperial scout vessel.
  • Explore suspected ruins beneath the ice cap.
  • Locate a missing scientific expedition funded by Dingir University.
