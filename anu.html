Imperial Survey Summary

Anu is the most important gas giant in the Dingir system. Its immense hydrogen reserves support one of the largest fuel-processing networks in the system. Hundreds of skimmers, fuel barges, refinery tenders, and maintenance craft operate within Anu's upper atmosphere and orbital infrastructure.

Traffic around Anu is heavy. Merchant vessels, scouts, naval patrols, corporate tankers, prospectors, passenger liners, and private yachts frequently pass through the Anu traffic zone.

Economic Importance

Anu provides refined fuel throughout the Dingir system and serves as one of the most important strategic assets controlled by the Imperial government.

Several noble houses and commercial interests maintain investments in the fuel industry surrounding Anu, creating a complex web of political influence.
