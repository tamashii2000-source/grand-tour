Imperial Survey Summary

Anu is the most important gas giant in the Dingir system. Its immense hydrogen reserves support one of the largest fuel-processing networks in the system. Hundreds of skimmers, fuel barges, refinery tenders, and maintenance craft operate in Anu's upper atmosphere and orbital infrastructure.

Traffic around Anu is heavy. Merchant ships, scouts, naval patrols, corporate tankers, prospectors, passenger liners, and private yachts frequently pass through the Anu traffic zone before continuing toward Dingir, Marduk, Nergal, or the outer system.

Major Installations

  • Anu Fuel Station Alpha — Largest orbital refinery in the Dingir system.
  • Imperial Naval Monitoring Station — Tracks ship traffic throughout the Anu moon network.
  • The Skimmer Yards — Orbital docks where atmospheric skimmers are built and maintained.
  • Customs Platform Twelve — Inspection point for fuel barges and moon-to-moon traffic.
  • Ringwatch Array — Sensor net hidden among Anu's inner ring particles.

Economic Importance

Anu's fuel infrastructure supports commercial travel throughout the Dingir system. Refined fuel from Anu supplies merchant traffic, naval patrols, industrial shipments, and long-distance vessels preparing for jump.

Control of Anu's fuel pricing is politically sensitive. Several noble houses, corporate interests, and Imperial offices have indirect influence over the fuel stations and refinery contracts.
