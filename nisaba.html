Imperial Survey Summary

Nisaba is an abandoned ice world located in the outer reaches of the Dingir system. Though uninhabited, the world remains strategically important due to the vast archive complexes buried beneath its northern ice sheets.

The extreme cold and stable geology make Nisaba one of the finest natural data-preservation sites in the sector. For centuries governments, noble families, corporations, universities, and intelligence agencies deposited records within secure vaults beneath the frozen surface.

Major Sites

  • Archive Prime — The largest data repository on the planet. Constructed during the early Imperial era.
  • The Vaults of Memory — Hundreds of sealed chambers containing historical records, legal documents, and cultural archives.
  • Station Sigma-11 — Automated communications relay that continues operating despite the disappearance of its maintenance crews.
  • The Frozen Library — Natural ice caverns containing pre-Imperial storage capsules discovered deep beneath the glacier.
  • The Black Vault — A restricted archive whose existence is officially denied by Imperial authorities.

Current Status

No permanent population exists on Nisaba. Visiting scholars, archivists, scout teams, and intelligence operatives arrive periodically to access specific records before departing again.

Most facilities are automated. Ancient maintenance systems continue to function despite centuries of neglect, and many sectors remain unexplored.

Historical Importance

The archives of Nisaba contain records dating back to the Rule of Man, early Imperial expansion, and the first settlement of the Dingir system. Many noble houses maintain private vaults beneath the ice.

Several succession disputes, trade agreements, and historical controversies have been settled through evidence recovered from Nisaba's repositories.

Adventure Hooks

  • A missing succession document could determine ownership of an entire planetary estate.
  • An intelligence team investigating Archive Prime has vanished without explanation.
  • A recently discovered vault contains records that contradict accepted Imperial history.
  • A self-aware AI may have hidden copies of itself within the archive network decades ago.
  • Someone has been altering historical records stored in multiple vaults.
  • An ancient pre-Imperial database is discovered beneath the Frozen Library.

Rumors
