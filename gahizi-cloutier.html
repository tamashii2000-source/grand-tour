Gahizi is a former Navy Academy graduate who became a pilot for the Imperial Ministry of State. He reached the rank of Petty Officer 1st Class and serves as a skilled pilot, sensor operator and backup astrogator.

Cloutier enjoys female companionship and has developed a reputation for having a "girl in every port."
