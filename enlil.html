Imperial Survey Summary

Enlil is one of Anu's inhabited moons and serves as a controlled agricultural colony within the Dingir system. Despite its cold climate and very thin tainted atmosphere, Enlil supports several million colonists through enclosed farming districts, heated valleys, algae vats, and low-gravity greenhouse complexes.

The world is administered by an externally imposed colonial authority. Officially, Enlil exists to guarantee food stability for smaller outposts and industrial holdings across the system. Unofficially, many local residents resent Dingir's control over export quotas, transport licenses, and infrastructure funding.

Major Locations

  • High Valley One — The largest enclosed agricultural basin on Enlil.
  • Grainhall Station — Central distribution hub for food exports.
  • The Frost Canals — Heated water channels linking greenhouse towns.
  • Old Colonial Landing — The original settlement site, now partly abandoned.
  • Governor's Dome — Administrative seat of the colonial authority.

Political Situation

Enlil's colonial government is loyal to Dingir, but local resentment is increasing. Farm cooperatives complain that they carry the burden of feeding the outer system while receiving poor starport maintenance, outdated equipment, and limited political representation.

The colony remains peaceful for now, but several workers' councils have begun meeting without official approval. Imperial observers have marked Enlil as a potential unrest zone.

Adventure Hooks
