Imperial Survey Summary

Geshtinanna is the outermost inhabited body in the Dingir system. Its remote location and low population make it an ideal site for scientific research, astronomical observation, and long-range sensor operations.

The world's small population consists primarily of scientists, technicians, survey crews, and support personnel. Most residents serve limited tours before returning to Dingir or other populated worlds.

Major Facilities

  • Farwatch Observatory — Largest astronomical observatory in the Dingir system.
  • Deep Sky Array — Massive sensor network scanning nearby interstellar space.
  • Survey Command Station — Headquarters for exploration missions beyond Dingir.
  • Listening Post Seven — Long-range communications and signal intelligence facility.
  • Outpost Horizon — Main settlement and starport complex.

Scientific Importance

The low level of electromagnetic interference makes Geshtinanna ideal for astronomical observations. Many discoveries regarding nearby star systems, rogue planets, and deep-space phenomena originated here.

The world also serves as the primary departure point for exploration missions into uncharted regions beyond the immediate borders of the Imperium.

Current Projects

  • Mapping nearby brown dwarf systems.
  • Monitoring unusual gravitational anomalies beyond the system edge.
  • Surveying potential future colony sites.
  • Tracking unexplained radio emissions detected in deep space.
  • Testing next-generation sensor technology.

Adventure Hooks

  • A deep-space probe transmits a distress signal from a system that should not exist.
  • A research team disappears while investigating a gravitational anomaly.
  • Listening Post Seven intercepts an encrypted signal from outside Imperial space.
  • A survey vessel returns with impossible navigational data.
  • An astronomer discovers evidence of a hidden base operating in the outer system.
  • Scientists request assistance investigating a mysterious object entering the system.

Rumors

  • Deep Sky Array has detected signs of intelligent activity beyond known space.
  • Listening Post Seven secretly reports directly to Imperial Intelligence.
  • Certain sensor readings have been classified above local clearance levels.
